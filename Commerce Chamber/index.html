About Us

We are a non-profit union organization eager to represent the businessmen of Arequipa, we promote the competitiveness of their companies, representing them seeking to improve their union interests and promoting their development.

We strengthen business activity, contributing to the integral development of small and medium-sized companies in our region.

Identified the problems that afflict the various social problems in general, promoting proposals and development projects that will change the situation and perspective of Arequipa.

COLCA VALLEY BEGINS TO RECEIVE MORE FOREIGN TOURISTS

In the last month, it was estimated that about 80 foreign visitors visited the Colca Valley who came to this destination of Arequipa.

The Colca Valley, located in the Arequipa province of Caylloma, begins to receive more foreign visitors, 14 months after the start of the pandemic that isolated the countries.

The representative of the Colca tourism entrepreneurs also highlighted the achievement of the Level 4 Hierarchy, which places the Colca Valley in an international context as attractive to visitors.

To enter the Colca Valley, national or foreign visitors must strictly comply with biosecurity measures, including handwashing and disinfection of vehicles that are carried out at the checkpoint upon entering Chivay.

By La Republica
