Design Principles

Ivette Soto

White Space and Clean Design

IBM.org

White Space and Clean Design that IBM contains direct the reader to important aspects of the company and the activities it offers. These spaces go along with the little text in the header and the text that accompanies the images.
Few words describe the activity of the organization.

Visual Hierarchy

NATGEO.com

National Geographic shows off visual hierarchy very well. The typography and size of the font are the way that they accomplish this.
The expectant images catch your attention first moment. The descriptions out on this page are done in a way to show that was designed so you would read first what they wanted you to read. And the button of subscribing is an attractor.

PARC: Repetition & Contrast

WINZARDING WORLD.com

My favorite web maintains a special contrast in the background colors and buttons, the text is added showing two very opposite fonts, the color of the text is repeated and maintains a contrast with the dark colors typical of the web page and images.
The images have a light color in the foreground to improve contrast.
