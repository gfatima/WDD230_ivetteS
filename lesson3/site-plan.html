Typography

Heading Font: Sanchez

The Sanchez typeface is strong and dramatic. The serifs draw attention and cannot be ignored. Seems great for headings.

Paragraph Font: Poppins

The Poppins typeface is light and not is difficult to read. The sans-serif style offers a suitable contrast.
