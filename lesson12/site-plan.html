Overview

Purpose

A website will be created for the municipality of Cerro colorado Arequipa. Our general web objective is to promote the commercial interests of the municipality and promote the area, attracting people and businesses to join our site.

Thus, the strengthening of all the commercial activity of our municipality will be achieved.

Likewise, it will be shown that the municipality of the Cerro Colorado Arequipa district is part of the great business activity of Arequipa, and is committed to supporting the increase in the competitiveness of the companies in the environment, it seeks to represent them by defending their interests and promoting their development

Audience

Who 1: Business owners, who wish to carry out development projects by changing their commercial situation and development perspective, as well as the municipality.

Who 2: Investors looking for well-connected thriving businesses in order to invest and generate profits.

Age: 12+

Needs and Expectations: Website with information that is easy to find and use. Clear and legible.

Income: Free access

Motivation: Give information to generate income

Paragraph Font: Comfortaa

The Comfortaa typeface is light and not is difficult to read. The sans-serif style offers a suitable contrast.
