Soda Springs Winter

by LakeTahoe

Soda Springs Winter Resort is located in the heart of the Northern Sierra on scenic Donner Summit. Ski, snowboard, snowshoe, sled and tube in a friendly atmosphere with an emphasis on family fun.

Soda Springs' spacious learning hill offers plenty of elbow room to learn and master new skills. Two chairlifts access 16 runs on wide-open terrain that is perfect for skiers and snowboarders of all levels. The terrain is rated 30% beginner, 50% intermediate and 20% advanced. The resort averages 400 inches of snowfall annually (576 inches last season). The summit elevation is 7,325 feet and the base elevation is 6,700 feet, making for a vertical drop of 652 feet. The longest run at Soda Springs Winter Resort is one mile.
